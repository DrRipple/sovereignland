Overview

Solborg is a medium-sized Scandinavian nation home to 20 million people. It is highly developed and runs off of a capitalist economy, combined with a strong welfare state. The country's largest trading partners are Norsolonia, Rhineland, and Amenembourg.

A majority of the government's funds go toward education and healthcare, contributing to the nation's high life expectancy and scores on the education index.
